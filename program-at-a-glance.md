## Design Notes for New Flyer

The existing Welcome Flyer (ScHack2026_WelcomeFlyer.pdf) covers:
- Event identity, venue & address
- Track rooms at Palazzo Venturi
- Opening session speakers
- Daily rhythm

A **second flyer** should show the full daily schedule as a compact grid/table, ideally fitting on A4 or A5. Suggested layout:
- Header: DANES ScHack 2026, dates, venue
- Central element: 5-column (Mon–Fri) × 3-row (morning / afternoon / evening) grid
- Each cell: track sessions summarized in 2–3 words per track
- Footer: website, contact, logos
