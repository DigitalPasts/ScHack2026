Registration

Applications are now open · Accepted participants notified within 2–3 weeks

If you have already pre-registered, your spot is secured — thank you! You still need to complete this full registration form and arrange payment of the €200 registration fee via the link provided in the form to confirm your place.

Key Information

Please complete the registration form below. Submission constitutes a formal application. Accepted participants will be notified within 2–3 weeks and will receive payment instructions for the registration fee.

  • Registration fee: €200, covering accommodation and meals for the full week
  • Places per track: Up to 10 students
  • No prior programming experience is required
  • Students are welcome to bring their own datasets and research questions
  • A certificate of completion will be issued to all participants
